# 村莊、建築與藍圖解析

村莊是 Millénaire 的靈魂，其核心邏輯在於建築的動態演進與資源循環。

## 🏘️ 村莊架構 (參考 `OldSource/java/org/millenaire/common/village/`)
- **`Building` (建築實體)：** `OldSource/java/org/millenaire/common/village/Building.java` (追蹤等級、資源及居住 NPC)
- **`BuildingLocation`：** `OldSource/java/org/millenaire/common/village/BuildingLocation.java` (坐標、朝向與範圍)
- **`Village` (村莊中心)：** `OldSource/java/org/millenaire/common/village/Village.java` (核心管理與聲望邏輯)
- **`BuildingManager`：** `OldSource/java/org/millenaire/common/village/buildingmanagers/` (施工任務協調)

## 📐 建築藍圖解析 (參考 `OldSource/java/org/millenaire/common/buildingplan/`)
- **藍圖解析器：** `OldSource/java/org/millenaire/common/buildingplan/BuildingPlan.java` (PNG 藍圖解析與升級路徑)
- **藍圖定義：** `OldSource/todeploy/millenaire/cultures/` (包含各文化的 `.txt` 建築定義與 PNG 藍圖)
- **`BuildingBlock`：** `OldSource/java/org/millenaire/common/buildingplan/BuildingBlock.java` (方塊元數據與文化變體)

## 🌍 世界生成與結構 (參考 `OldSource/java/org/millenaire/common/world/`)
- **村莊生成：** `OldSource/java/org/millenaire/common/world/MillWorldData.java`
- **坐標工具：** `src/main/java/me/devupdates/millenaireReborn/common/util/Point.java` (現代化 3D 坐標點實作)
