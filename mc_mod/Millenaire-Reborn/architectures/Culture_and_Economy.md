# Cultures & Economy

Millénaire features several distinct historical cultures, each with its own aesthetics, technology, and economic systems.

## 🌏 Cultures (`common.culture`)
Supported cultures include:
- **Norman**: 11th-century French culture (Stone & Wood buildings, wheat/cider).
- **Byzantine**: Eastern Roman Empire (Grand architecture, silk, wine).
- **Japanese**: Heian-era Japan (Rice, sake, specialized weaponry).
- **Mayan**: Classic Period Maya (Corn, cocoa, stone pyramids).
- **Indian**: Medieval Indian cultures (Rice, spices, brick architecture).
- **Seljuk**: Seljuk Turks (Desert architecture, nomadic roots).
- **Inuit**: Arctic culture (Ice/snow structures, fishing/hunting).

## 💰 Economy & Trading
- **Denier**: The custom currency system with three tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold).
- **Marketplace**: Villagers trade resources with each other and the player.
- **Community Chests**: Central storage for village-wide resources used for construction and trade.
- **Player Reputation**: Actions (trading, helping, raiding) affect the player's status in a village, unlocking new trade options or attracting hostile guards.

## 📜 Quest System
- **Creation Quests**: Major questlines that delve into the lore of each culture.
- **Village Tasks**: Small-scale requests from villagers (e.g., "Bring 10 wood", "Kill the nearby monsters").
- **Rewards**: Unique items, reputation boosts, and village upgrades.
